Webmii is a search engine focused on people, allowing you to find publicly available information about individuals across the internet. When you search, it provides a "visibility index" that reflects the online presence of the person. The service is accessible through its official website and also offers a mobile app available on the App Store. Additionally, Webmii has a desktop extension that works on both Mac and Windows, providing a more integrated search experience.
